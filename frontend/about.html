✨ Welcome to TravelMate

TravelMate is your personal AI-powered travel assistant. We built it to help people like you plan amazing trips — without the stress. Whether you're exploring a new city or dreaming of a peaceful vacation, TravelMate makes it easier, smarter, and more fun.

🌍 What TravelMate Does

TravelMate helps you plan trips effortlessly using AI. You can:

  • 🧠 Get personalized destination suggestions based on your interests, budget, group size, and trip duration.
  • 📅 Generate full day-by-day itineraries instantly.
  • 🌦️ View live weather forecasts to prepare better.
  • 🗺️ Discover real locations, food spots, and activities — all AI-curated just for you.

🛠️ How It Works

It’s simple! Just fill out a short form with your preferences:

  1. 📝 Trip type, interests, budget, group, and travel dates
  2. ⚙️ Our AI understands your needs and generates realistic recommendations
  3. 🗓️ You get a detailed itinerary with local sights, food suggestions, and tips
  4. 🌤️ Live weather is shown using OpenWeatherMap
  5. 📸 Each destination includes beautiful images from Unsplash

🛣️ The Development Journey

TravelMate started as a passion project:

  • ✏️ From sketches and wireframes to a polished web app
  • 💻 Built using HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript
  • 🔐 Firebase Authentication added for secure login
  • 🤖 Integrated OpenRouter AI for travel suggestions
  • 🌦️ Weather via OpenWeatherMap API
  • 🎨 Designed to be smooth, clean, and user-friendly

🔮 Future Plans

  • 👥 Community trip sharing and inspiration board
  • 📱 Launching a mobile app for better accessibility
  • 🧬 Advanced AI with more personalized suggestions
  • 📶 Offline support for travel without internet
  • 🕶️ VR previews of popular destinations
